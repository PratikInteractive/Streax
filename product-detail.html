100% Grey Coverage

Achieve 100% grey coverage with this gel hair colour, designed for effectively coloured and glossy strands. The formula delivers a uniform, vibrant, and natural result, ensuring no grey hair is visible.

Long Lasting

Gel hair colour offers up to 20 washes of long-lasting vibrancy and shine. Its durable formulation maintains rich colour, extending your new look before a touch-up is needed.

Step 1: Preparation

Start with dry, unwashed hair. Wear the gloves provided and apply petroleum jelly around your hairline and ears to prevent staining.

Step 2: Mixing

Mix the colour gel and developer in the provided applicator bottle. Shake gently until well blended.

Step 3: Application

Section your hair and apply the mixture evenly from roots to ends. Make sure all strands are thoroughly coated.

Step 4: Processing & Rinsing

Leave the colour on for 30-35 minutes. Rinse with water until it runs clear, then use the provided conditioner.
